# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Board Decision Recovery Readiness Brief is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- convert fragile board-facing decision paths into named recovery priorities
- show where redundancy, fallback ownership, or friction removal will buy the next clean cycle after a lane breaks
- tie restore weakness and fallback coverage directly to board confidence instead of abstract governance language
- convert scattered recovery bottlenecks into one reusable recovery-readiness packet for boards and investors
